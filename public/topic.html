The finest Porsche. More innovative than ever.



The new Porsche 911 GT3 RS is the first Porsche street car to have a fully optimized drag-reduction system, proportionized by the huge rear wing, which has never been used before.

The engine is a high-revving naturally aspirated one with 525 horsepower, slightly less powerful than the GT2 RS; the difference lies in the curves. And not just the curves on the track,

but also the curves of the car. Its design is extremely unique and beautiful, which is the reason behind the huge liking and popularity of the car since its release.
